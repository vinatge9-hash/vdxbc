Our Story

We exist to make quality dog care accessible, ethical, and joyful.

Mission & Values

Paws & Care was founded by dog lovers and certified professionals. We believe every dog deserves compassionate, knowledgeable care delivered in a safe environment. We prioritize positive training, cleanliness, and individualized attention.

  • • Certified staff & trained handlers
  • • Clean, supervised play areas
  • • Positive reinforcement training

How we care

  1. Meet & greet: We assess temperament and needs.
  2. Individual plans: Feeding, rest, and activity preferences noted.
  3. Daily updates: Photos and notes available to owners.
  4. Ongoing training: Behavior-focused sessions to build skills.
